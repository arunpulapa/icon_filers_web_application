FBAR & FATCA Compliance

Stay compliant with US law: required foreign bank account and asset reporting for all US citizens and resident aliens.
FBAR Filing (TD F 90-22.1) FATCA Reporting (Form 8938)

Who Must File?

  • US citizens and resident aliens with >$10,000 in foreign accounts (FBAR).
  • >$50,000 in assets (bank/securities/deposits) must also file FATCA Form 8938.
  • Annual deadline: April 15th. Avoid penalties—file on time.
