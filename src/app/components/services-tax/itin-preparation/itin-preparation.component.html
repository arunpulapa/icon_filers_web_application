Certified Acceptance Agent (CAA)
  1. IRS–authorized Certified Acceptance Agent services.
  2. Passport verification and identity confirmation.
  3. Documentation certification for ITIN applications.
  4. Issuance of Certificate of Accuracy (COA) to the IRS, reducing the chance of rejection.
  5. Coordination with IRS ITIN Operations Office until your ITIN is processed.
Who Needs an ITIN?
Nonresident and resident aliens, spouses and dependents who are not eligible for an SSN but have U.S. tax filing or reporting requirements.
No SSN Required
ITIN is a tax processing number only – it does not change your immigration status or authorize work.
