Partnership Tax Return (Form 1065)

For two or more owners who share profits and losses. We prepare the partnership return plus K-1s for each partner.

  • K-1 preparation for all partners.
  • Income, expenses and allocations by ownership %.
  • Capital accounts, contributions and distributions.
  • Support for new and converting partnerships.

Limited Liability Company (LLC)

Whether your LLC is taxed as a disregarded entity, partnership or S-Corp, we file the correct return and keep your books clean.

  • Single-member (Schedule C) and multi-member (1065) filings.
  • Bookkeeping tidy-up and expense categorisation.
  • Separate tracking of member draws vs. payroll.
  • Guidance on when to elect S-Corporation status.

C-Corporation (Form 1120)

Ideal for companies planning to reinvest profits or raise capital. We keep your corporate return fully compliant.

  • Form 1120 with balance sheet and P&L schedules.
  • Officer compensation and dividend planning.
  • Depreciation, Section 179 and bonus depreciation review.
  • Multi-state filings and apportionment where required.

S-Corporation (Form 1120S)

Popular with small businesses seeking payroll + distribution flexibility. We help maintain reasonable compensation and clean K-1s.

  • 1120S preparation with shareholder K-1s.
  • Reasonable salary vs. distributions guidance.
  • Tracking retained earnings and shareholder basis.
  • Help with S-Election (Form 2553) where applicable.

Sole Proprietorship (Schedule C)

For freelancers and small business owners who file with their individual return. We make sure you claim every legitimate deduction.

  • Schedule C preparation from your bank/expense records.
  • Home office, vehicle, travel and software expenses.
  • Guidance on separating personal and business spending.
  • Quarterly estimated tax calculations and reminders.
