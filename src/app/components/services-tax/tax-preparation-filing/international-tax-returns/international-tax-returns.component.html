Foreign Tax Credits (Form 1116)

For U.S. taxpayers paying tax abroad, we help you avoid double taxation by accurately claiming foreign tax credits.

  • Classify foreign income by category (general, passive, etc.).
  • Compute limitation and carryover rules for Form 1116.
  • Coordinate with foreign tax returns to maximize benefit.
  • Handle multiple countries and currency conversions.

Foreign Owned U.S. Entities (1120 / 5472)

Required reporting for U.S. corporations owned by foreign shareholders, including related-party transactions.

  • Form 1120 preparation for the U.S. corporation.
  • Form 5472 reporting of foreign ownership & transactions.
  • Documentation of loans, management fees & cost sharing.
  • Record-keeping guidance to support transfer pricing.

Foreign Corporations (1120-F / 5471)

For foreign companies with U.S. activities or U.S. shareholders, we prepare complex information returns and disclosures.

  • Form 1120-F for foreign corporations with U.S. ECI.
  • Form 5471 for U.S. shareholders of CFCs or foreign corps.
  • Ownership, sub-part F and GILTI calculations (as applicable).
  • Co-ordination with foreign advisors on source & treaty issues.

Foreign Partnerships (Form 8865)

Reporting for U.S. persons who own or control foreign partnerships or joint ventures.

  • Form 8865 preparation with required schedules.
  • Track ownership percentages and profit-sharing.
  • Report capital accounts, contributions and distributions.
  • Coordinate with Form 1116 / 1118 where credits apply.

Foreign Branches (Form 8858)

For U.S. businesses operating through foreign branches or disregarded entities, we prepare the required branch reporting.

  • Form 8858 for each foreign branch or DE.
  • Segregation of branch income, assets and taxes paid.
  • Integration with FTC calculations and Form 1118/1116.
  • Guidance on restructuring branches vs. subsidiaries.
