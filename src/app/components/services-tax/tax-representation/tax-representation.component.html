How It Works
  1. You receive an IRS or state tax notice or audit letter.
  2. You share the notice and authorize us via Power of Attorney (Form 2848).
  3. We review your returns, books and documents and prepare the response strategy.
  4. Our enrolled agent / EA/CPA communicates directly with the IRS or state on your behalf.
  5. We keep you updated until the case is resolved and provide guidance for future compliance.
