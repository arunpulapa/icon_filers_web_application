Compliance under Corporate Transparency Act (CTA)

We review your entity structure and owners to determine exactly what has to be reported under the new CTA rules.

  • Identify reporting companies that fall under CTA rules.
  • Review beneficial owners, control persons and exemptions.
  • Explain due dates and penalties for non-compliance.
  • Prepare a clear action plan for your business group.

Customized Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) Report

We collect owner data, build a clean BOI report and prepare it in the exact format required by FinCEN.

  • Gather identity, ownership and control details securely.
  • Map complex structures (multi-member, foreign owners, etc.).
  • Prepare FinCEN-ready BOI report and internal summary.
  • Store owner info for future updates and audits.

Submission & Confirmation from FinCEN

We file your BOI report through the FinCEN system and track the official confirmation.

  • Electronic submission of BOI report to FinCEN.
  • Obtain and store submission receipts / IDs.
  • Share confirmation PDF for your internal records.
  • Assist with corrections if FinCEN flags any issues.

Ongoing BOI Report Update Filings

Ownership and control often change; we manage required BOI updates so you remain compliant year-round.

  • Monitor trigger events: ownership, address, or officer changes.
  • Prepare and submit updated BOI filings on time.
  • Maintain a change log for regulators and auditors.
  • Coordinate updates with tax, legal and state filings.
