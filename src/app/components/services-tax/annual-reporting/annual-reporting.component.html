What We Cover

  • Annual report and franchise filings with the Secretary of State.

Our Annual Compliance Process
  1. Collect entity details: states of registration, owners and activity status.
  2. Build a compliance calendar for annual reports, franchise taxes and key deadlines.
  3. Remind you ahead of time and prepare required filings or data sheets.
  4. Submit annual reports and renewals online or by mail where required.
  5. Track confirmations and keep digital records for future reference and audits.
